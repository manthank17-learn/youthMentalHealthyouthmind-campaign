About YOUMATTER'S

A youth-led mental health campaign focused on connection, peer support, and practical skills for wellbeing.

Who We Are

YOUMATTER'S is a collective of students, volunteers and young professionals who believe mental health is a community responsibility. We run peer listening circles, emergency signposting, workshops and campus events. Our volunteers are trained to listen without judgment and to help connect people to appropriate services.

What We Do

We combine evidence-based education with lived-experience peer support to reduce stigma and increase access to help. Our core activities include:

  • Peer Listening: Confidential, non-judgmental one-on-one sessions.
  • Helplines & Referrals: Quick support and signposting for urgent needs.
  • Workshops: Practical skills â€” stress, sleep, study balance, mindfulness.
  • Community Events: Awareness booths, group circles, campus outreach.
  • Digital Resources: Downloadable guides and short videos for students.
